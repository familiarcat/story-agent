## Quark's economics (crew vs SaaS)

- **SaaS path:** per-seat licenses across Dealpath/Northspyre/AppFolio/MRI etc. — recurring, per-user,
  data siloed in each vendor.
- **Crew path:** we build *owned* systems + run agentic workflows on **cost-optimized routing**
  (cheapest-adequate model per task), with **cost measured per mission** and knowledge **compounding
  in your RAG** instead of locked in vendors.
- **The honest framing:** SaaS is faster to switch on for commodity ops; the crew wins where you want
  *owned IP, integrated data, and automation of the judgment work* (sourcing/underwriting/reporting) —
  and on TCO at scale. We'll put a real **cost-per-capability table** next to your actual deal volume
  once we see the PDF.
